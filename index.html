Wizards

About Wizards

Wizards are meticulous spellcasters who learn their magic through rigorous study and mastery of arcane knowledge. With a spellbook in hand, they have unmatched versatility and can adapt to any challenge.

Wizard Subclasses

  • Evocation: Masters of destructive spells, they unleash powerful elemental energy while sparing allies.
  • Divination: Prophets of fate, they peer into the future to alter outcomes in their favor.
  • Necromancy: Commanders of the undead, they sap life from foes and empower their allies.

Wizard-Exclusive Spells

  • Mage Hand Ledgerdemain: An advanced version of Mage Hand, allowing you to manipulate objects at range with precision.
  • Counterspell: Instantly nullify an enemy's spellcasting attempt. A game-changer in magical duels.
  • Simulacrum: Create a near-perfect duplicate of a creature, doubling your effectiveness.

Sorcerers

About Sorcerers

Sorcerers are innate spellcasters whose magic comes from their lineage or a mystical event. Their spellcasting is spontaneous, relying on instinct rather than study.

Sorcerer Subclasses

  • Draconic Bloodline: Infused with the power of dragons, granting resilience and elemental mastery.
  • Wild Magic: Unpredictable magic that can turn the tide of battle... or create chaos.
  • Storm Sorcery: Harnessing the power of storms, they control weather and lightning with ease.

Sorcerer-Exclusive Spells

  • Chaos Bolt: A wildly unpredictable spell that leaps between targets, changing damage types.
  • Twinned Spell (Metamagic): Cast the same spell on two targets at once for devastating effects.
  • Dragon's Breath: Temporarily imbue yourself or an ally with a dragon's fiery breath weapon.

Shared Spells Ranked

  1. FIREBALL - Iconic area damage spell. Clears out mobs with fiery precision.
  2. HASTE - Boosts speed, grants extra actions, and enhances defense. A must-have for your team.
  3. MAGIC MISSILE - Unerring bolts of force that never miss. Reliable and deadly.
  4. SHIELD - Instant magical defense that turns a near-hit into a miss.
  5. MISTY STEP - Quick teleportation to reposition or escape trouble.
